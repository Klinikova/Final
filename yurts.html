The Yurts at Pacific Trail

What is yurt?

Our luxury yurts are permanent structures four feet off the ground. Each yurt is fully enclosed with canvas walls, a wooden floor, and a roof dome that can be open.

How are the yurts furnished?

Each yurt is furnished with a queen-size bed with down quilt and gas-fired stove. Your luxury camping experience includes electricity and a sink with hot and cold running water.

What should I bring?

Most guests pack comfortable walking shoes and plan to dress for charming weather with light layers of clothing. It's also helpful to bring a flashlight and a sense of adventure!
